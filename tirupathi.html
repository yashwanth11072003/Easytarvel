Tirumala Tirupathi

Places to visit

Tirupati is a major pilgrimage city in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India, known for the Sri Venkateswara Temple located on the Tirumala Hills. If you're interested in exploring nearby places from Tirupati, here are some suggestions:
1. Sri Venkateswara Temple (Tirumala): This temple, also known as the Tirupati Balaji Temple, is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, a form of Lord Vishnu. It is one of the most visited and revered temples in India.
2. Tiruchanur: Located near Tirupati, Tiruchanur is known for the Padmavathi Ammavari Temple, dedicated to Goddess Padmavathi, the consort of Lord Venkateswara.
3. Sri Kalyana Venkateswara Swamy Temple (Srinivasa Mangapuram): Situated near Tirupati, this temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara and is often visited by newlyweds seeking the deity's blessings.
4. ISKCON Tirupati (Sri Sri Radha Govinda Mandir): The International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) has a presence in Tirupati, and the temple is dedicated to Lord Krishna.
5. Chandragiri Fort and Palace: About 16 kilometers from Tirupati, Chandragiri Fort and Palace have historical significance and offer panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
6. Talakona Waterfalls: Located around 49 kilometers from Tirupati, Talakona Waterfalls is the highest waterfall in the Andhra Pradesh state. It's a scenic spot surrounded by lush greenery.
7. Kanipakam Vinayaka Temple: Situated about 75 kilometers from Tirupati, this temple is known for the self-manifested idol of Lord Ganesha, and it attracts a significant number of devotees.
