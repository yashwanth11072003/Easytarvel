Vaishno Devi Temple

Places to visit

The Vaishno Devi Temple is a prominent Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi, located in the Trikuta Mountains of the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir. If you're interested in exploring nearby places from the Vaishno Devi Temple, here are some suggestions:
1. Ardhkuwari Mata Temple: Situated along the way to the Vaishno Devi Temple, Ardhkuwari is known for the cave temple dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi in her half-woman and half-lion form.
2. Bhairon Temple: At the end of the trek to Vaishno Devi, the Bhairon Temple is dedicated to Bhairon, a form of Lord Shiva. Pilgrims often visit this temple as part of the yatra (pilgrimage).
3. Banganga Temple: Located in Katra, the base camp for the Vaishno Devi pilgrimage, Banganga Temple is dedicated to the Goddess and is situated near the Banganga River.
4. Charan Paduka Temple: It is believed to be the place where Goddess Vaishno Devi's footprints are imprinted. Pilgrims often visit this spot during the yatra.
5. Shiv Khori Cave Temple: Located around 70 kilometers from Katra, this cave temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva. The cave is adorned with various naturally formed structures resembling deities.
6. Raghunath Temple, Jammu: About 50 kilometers from Katra, the Raghunath Temple is one of the largest temple complexes in northern India, dedicated to Lord Rama.
7. Kishtwar National Park: If you are a nature enthusiast, Kishtwar National Park is around 150 kilometers from Katra and offers diverse flora and fauna, along with stunning landscapes.
