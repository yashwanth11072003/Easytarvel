Lingaraj Temple

Places to visit

The Lingaraj Temple is a significant Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and is located in the capital city of Odisha, Bhubaneswar, India. If you're interested in exploring nearby places from the Lingaraj Temple, here are some suggestions:
1. Bindusagar Tank: Located near Lingaraj Temple, Bindusagar is a large water tank that is considered sacred. Devotees often take a dip in the tank before visiting the Lingaraj Temple.
2. Mukteshwar Temple: This temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is renowned for its exquisite architecture and is situated not far from Lingaraj Temple. It's a fine example of Kalinga architecture.
3. Rajarani Temple: Known for its intricate carvings and beautiful sculptures, the Rajarani Temple is another architectural gem in Bhubaneswar. It's a short distance from Lingaraj Temple.
4. Kedar Gouri Temple: Dedicated to Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, the Kedar Gouri Temple is an ancient temple located near the Lingaraj Temple. It's known for its historical significance.
5. Odisha State Museum: If you're interested in history and culture, the Odisha State Museum in Bhubaneswar is worth a visit. It houses a vast collection of artifacts, sculptures, and paintings.
6. Dhauli Shanti Stupa: Located about 8 kilometers from Bhubaneswar, the Dhauli Shanti Stupa is a Buddhist stupa built on the hills of Dhauli. It commemorates the Kalinga War and promotes peace.
7. Nandankanan Zoological Park: Situated on the outskirts of Bhubaneswar, Nandankanan is a popular zoo and botanical garden. It is home to a variety of wildlife, including white tigers.
