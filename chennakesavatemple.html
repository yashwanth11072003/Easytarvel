Chenna Kesava Temple

Places to visit

The Chennakesava Temple is a famous temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is located in the town of Belur, Karnataka, India. It is renowned for its exquisite Hoysala architecture and intricate carvings. If you're interested in exploring nearby places from the Chennakesava Temple in Belur, here are some suggestions:
1. Halebidu: Another significant Hoysala temple town, Halebidu is located about 16 kilometers from Belur. The Hoysaleswara Temple in Halebidu is known for its detailed carvings and impressive architecture.
2. Shravanabelagola: This town is known for the gigantic monolithic statue of Lord Gommateshwara Bahubali. Shravanabelagola is about 80 kilometers from Belur and is an important Jain pilgrimage site.
3. Yagachi Dam: Located near Belur, the Yagachi Dam offers a serene setting with a reservoir and lush green surroundings. It's a peaceful place to visit, particularly for nature lovers.
4. Javagal: If you're interested in exploring historical sites, Javagal is known for its ancient temples and is located not far from Belur.
5. Hassan: The nearby town of Hassan is known for its historical and cultural significance. You can explore the Hasanamba Temple and other attractions in the town.
6. Gorur Dam: Situated on the Hemavati River, Gorur Dam is a picturesque spot near Hassan. The dam offers scenic views and is a nice place for a relaxing visit.
