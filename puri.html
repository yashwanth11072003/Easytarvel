Puri Jagannath Temple

Places to visit

Puri is a city located in the state of Odisha, India, and it is known for its famous Jagannath Temple, which is a significant Hindu pilgrimage site. If you're interested in exploring nearby places from Puri, here are some suggestions:
1. Jagannath Temple: This iconic temple is the main attraction in Puri and is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, along with his siblings Balabhadra and Subhadra. The annual Rath Yatra (chariot festival) held here is a major religious event.
2. Puri Beach: The Puri Beach is a popular destination for both pilgrims and tourists. It's a great place to enjoy the sea, take a stroll along the shore, and witness beautiful sunrise and sunset views.
3. Konark Sun Temple: Located approximately 35 kilometers from Puri, the Konark Sun Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Dedicated to the Sun God, it is known for its architectural grandeur and the intricately carved stone wheels.
4. Chilika Lake: About an hour's drive from Puri, Chilika Lake is the largest coastal lagoon in India. It's a haven for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts. Boat rides are available to explore the lake and its islands.
5. Gundicha Temple: This temple is associated with the Rath Yatra and is located in the middle of the Puri town. During the festival, Lord Jagannath, along with his siblings, stays here for a week.
6. Chilika Wildlife Sanctuary: The Chilika Wildlife Sanctuary, located along the shores of Chilika Lake, is home to a variety of migratory birds and is a designated Ramsar site.
7. Raghurajpur Artist Village: Situated about 14 kilometers from Puri, Raghurajpur is a traditional crafts village known for its Pattachitra paintings, palm leaf engravings, and other handicrafts.
