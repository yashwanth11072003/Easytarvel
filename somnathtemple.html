Somnath Temple

Places to visit

Somnath Temple is one of the most important and revered temples in India, dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is located in the town of Veraval in the state of Gujarat, on the western coast of India. If you're interested in exploring nearby places from Somnath Temple, here are some suggestions:
1. Triveni Sangam: A short distance from Somnath Temple, Triveni Sangam is the confluence of three holy rivers – Hiran, Kapila, and Saraswati. Pilgrims often take a ritualistic dip at this sacred spot.
2. Bhalka Tirtha: Located near Veraval, Bhalka Tirtha is believed to be the place where Lord Krishna was mistakenly hit by an arrow, leading to his departure from the mortal world. The site has a temple dedicated to Lord Krishna.
3. Prabhas Patan Museum: Situated near Somnath, this museum houses artifacts and sculptures from the ancient period, providing insights into the rich history and cultural heritage of the region.
4. Gita Mandir: Located in Somnath, the Gita Mandir is a temple dedicated to Lord Krishna and is known for its intricately carved marble pillars depicting verses from the Bhagavad Gita.
5. Junagadh: A historic city near Somnath, Junagadh is home to various historical and religious sites, including Uparkot Fort, Mahabat Maqbara, and Girnar Hill, known for its Jain temples.
6. Sasan Gir National Park: About 40 kilometers from Somnath, Sasan Gir is the only place in the world where you can spot Asiatic lions in their natural habitat. The national park is also home to diverse flora and fauna.
7. Diu: A coastal town located near Somnath, Diu is known for its Portuguese architecture, beautiful beaches, and historic sites, including Diu Fort.
