Siddhi Vinayak Temple

Places to visit

The Siddhivinayak Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha and is located in Prabhadevi, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. If you're interested in exploring nearby places from Siddhivinayak Temple, here are some suggestions:
1. Mahalakshmi Temple: Located nearby, the Mahalakshmi Temple is dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi, the goddess of wealth and prosperity. It's situated on the banks of the Arabian Sea.
2. Worli Sea Face: Enjoy a scenic drive or stroll along Worli Sea Face, offering views of the Arabian Sea and the Worli skyline. It's a popular spot for locals and tourists alike.
3. Bandra-Worli Sea Link: A modern engineering marvel, the Bandra-Worli Sea Link is a cable-stayed bridge that connects the suburbs of Bandra and Worli. The bridge offers stunning views of the Mumbai coastline.
4. Shree Swayambhu Ganpati Temple: Located in Prabhadevi, not far from Siddhivinayak Temple, this temple is dedicated to Lord Ganesha. It's known for its self-manifested idol of Lord Ganpati.
5. Nehru Science Centre: If you're interested in science and technology, the Nehru Science Centre in Worli is a popular science museum with interactive exhibits and activities.
6. Kamala Nehru Park: Situated in Malabar Hill, this park offers panoramic views of the city and the Arabian Sea. It's a peaceful place to relax and enjoy the surroundings.
