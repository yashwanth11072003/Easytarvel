Akshardham Temple

Places to visit

Akshardham is a major cultural and spiritual complex in Delhi, India. If you're looking for nearby places to visit from Akshardham, here are some suggestions:
1. India Gate: A prominent landmark in Delhi, India Gate is a war memorial dedicated to soldiers who died in World War I. It's a short distance from Akshardham and is surrounded by beautiful lawns.
2. Lotus Temple: Known for its unique lotus-shaped design, the Lotus Temple is a Bahá'í House of Worship. It's relatively close to Akshardham and is renowned for its serene environment.
3. Swaminarayan Akshardham Yogi Hraday Kamal: Located near Akshardham, this park is dedicated to the founder of the Swaminarayan tradition and offers a peaceful setting for relaxation.
4. National Zoological Park: If you enjoy wildlife, the National Zoological Park in Delhi is not far from Akshardham. It's a great place for a family outing.
5. Chandni Chowk: This bustling market area is known for its vibrant atmosphere, street food, and historical charm. It's a bit farther from Akshardham but worth a visit for a unique shopping and dining experience.
