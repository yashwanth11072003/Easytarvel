Ayodhya Ram Mandir

Places to visit

Ayodhya is surrounded by several places of interest: 1. Naimisharanya (Nimsar): A sacred forest mentioned in Hindu scriptures, believed to be where sages performed yagnas.
2. Faizabad: The twin city of Ayodhya, with historical sites like Faizabad Fort and Gulab Bari.
3. Choti Chhawni: A historical spot believed to be Lord Rama's military headquarters with ruins and ancient structures.
4. Ramkot: Situated on the banks of the Saryu River, considered the exact spot where Lord Rama lived.
5. Treta Ke Thakur: A holy temple dedicated to Lord Rama, attracting pilgrims throughout the year.
6. Kanak Bhawan: A temple known for intricate architecture, dedicated to Lord Rama and Sita.
7. Janki Mahal: Located in Faizabad, a temple dedicated to Goddess Sita with paintings and artifacts depicting the Ramayana.
